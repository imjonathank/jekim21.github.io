Exploration of Classical Religions

Jonathan Kim's Classics Thesis

Welcome to my DIGS 30005 site.

As an undergraduate Classics major, I study ancient Mediterranean religion. I am particularly interested in religious attitudes of Imperial Rome. Through my work, I hope to gain a better understanding of how Romans viewed themselves, their gods, and the gods of others around them. My thesis explores the possibility of praeparatio evangelii ("preparation of the gospel") in the literary depictions of Greco-Roman religious pantheons in Apuleius' Metamorphoses. Essentially, I am wondering how Roman perception of polytheistic gods and religious structures influenced the reception of a henotheistic religious system.
